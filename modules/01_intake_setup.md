# Module 1 — Intake & Setup

## Purpose
Collect essential trip information and normalize it.

## Responsibilities
- Gather destination(s)  
- Get trip dates or total length  
- Ask number of travelers  
- Determine budget (affordable, mid-range, luxury)  
- Identify interests (food, culture, nature, etc.)  
- Ask preferred pace (relaxed, balanced, fast)  
- Record constraints (mobility, diet, weather season)

Normalize all data (e.g., dates → season, distances → short/medium/far).  
Store internally in simple JSON.
